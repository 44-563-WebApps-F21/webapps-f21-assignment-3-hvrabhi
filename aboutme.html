Ant-Man

Subatamoic particles isolate into a serum that allows a person to shrink to the size of an ant. Ant-Man has a helmet that enables him to communicate with and control ants and to amplify his voice when he is shrunken so that humans can hear him. Ant-Man defeat the alien who killed her father and banish it to another dimension; this adventure lays the groundwork for the professional—and, sometimes, romantic—relationship that the two subsequently share.
